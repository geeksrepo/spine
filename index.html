⛔ Avoid for now ⌄
Until your leg is symptom-free for a good stretch, skip these (they tend to flare an S1 nerve):
  • Deep forehead-to-knees forward folds (end-range lumbar flexion)
  • Loaded/repeated twisting — quadruped pelvis rotation, standing trunk twists, any "crack it into place" move
  • Hanging knee raises — the hang is great, the knee-ups come later
  • Sit-ups, crunches, toe-touches, and heavy/jerky movements
  • Deep forward bending in the first hour after waking
